Frequently asked questions

How do I rent movies from Muvi?

Find your movie of choice and click on the 'Rent movie' button.

Can I browse movies based on a specific category?

Yes! Go to movies and click on categories. Then select your desired category/genre.

What payment options are available?

We currently accept payments via PayPal and credit/debit cards.

Are the movies shipped physically or streamed online?

They are streamed online in 1080p quality. Some titles are even available in 3D!

Who are we?

Muvi is a web page created for a project in IT2805. This web page aims to give you the pleasure of watching movies in high quality and maintain its "easy to use" interface. Although you cannot really rent movies here at the moment, we do deliver valid information about the movies we currently have in our system. Our goal is to beat every competitor in the field of online movies. We believe that we have the potential to beat them on price, quality and customer service.
